Also, I have been trying to keep up with ICPC meetings and the challenges they put out but I don't think I

I was doing the same full body gym routine for almost two years up until recently. I was seeing great
results with this routine, but my hips starting hurting and I found it was no longer sustainable,
probably due to the amount of squatting I was doing. I decided about two weeks ago to switch to a
foundations-type exercise where you hold a squat position with no weight for up to five minutes. So
far, I have only been able to hold it for 2 minutes, but my lower body already feels better and more
coordinated. The rest of my workout remains relatively the same. We will see how this fairs in the
long run.
